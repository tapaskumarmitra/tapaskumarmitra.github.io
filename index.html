ABOUT

About Tapas

Tapas Kumar Mitra is a distinguished legal professional with over 17 years of dedicated service in the judiciary of West Bengal & 22+ years in legal practice. Having presided over courts across multiple districts of the state, he has earned a reputation for delivering well-reasoned, thorough, and timely judgements in both civil and criminal matters.

His judicial career culminated in the position of Additional District & Sessions Judge at Fast Track Courts, where he handled high-stakes criminal trials, sessions cases, and complex civil disputes with diligence and integrity. His postings across diverse districts — from Hooghly and Nadia to North 24 Parganas and Purulia — gave him a comprehensive understanding of the legal challenges faced by people across West Bengal.

Now retired from the bench and practicing as an Advocate, Tapas Kumar Mitra offers legal consultation, case analysis, and representation through Mitra Legal Services. His deep knowledge of procedural law, criminal law, and civil law — combined with his first-hand experience of judicial decision-making — provides a unique advantage for his clients.
